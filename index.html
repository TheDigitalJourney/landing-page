Frequently asked

Questions we always get

Is PERITO IBP a replacement for SAP APO?
Yes. With SAP APO mainstream support ending in 2027, many mid-market manufacturers are looking for alternatives to the complex, expensive SAP IBP migration. PERITO IBP is ERP-agnostic and provides a powerful, faster-to-implement alternative designed specifically for mid-market supply chains.
Do we have to replace our current ERP?
Absolutely not. PERITO IBP is a cloud-native platform that sits on top of your existing ERP, MES, and SCADA systems. It acts as the strategic intelligence layer, feeding optimized plans back into your execution systems.
How does the Sustainable Supply Chain module work?
Unlike separate ESG reporting tools, PERITO IBP integrates real-time emission data directly into operational planning. This allows planners to prioritize sea freight over air freight, consolidate orders, and balance Scope 3 carbon emission reductions with profitability during the actual S&OP process.
What does 'fenced' Demand AI mean?
AI can hallucinate. Our Demand AI uses deep learning to detect hidden patterns, but we 'fence' it with strict, rule-based validation. This ensures your planners stay in control, focusing on strategy rather than fixing AI anomalies.
